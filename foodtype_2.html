Description

This delicious hot dog omelette has been slow cooked until perfection. Serves 1-2 people.

Needed items

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Hot dog
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Pan

Cooking steps

  1. Warm the pan on a stove with low to medium heat
  2. Add the butter and hot dog
  3. Cooked the hot dog until browned
  4. Add the eggs
  5. Stir until eggs are thoroughly cooked
  6. Serve and eat
