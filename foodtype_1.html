Description

This is a delicious bowl of cereal. This recipe will teach you how to make sure the cereal has the right amount of crunch. Serves 1

Needed items

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Eating utensil

Cooking steps

  1. Place your bowl on a flat surface with the rim facing up
  2. Pour the cereal halfway into the bowl
  3. Pour the milk about half inch above the cereal
  4. Let the cereal settle for about 2 minutes
  5. Grab your eating utensil and enjoy your food
