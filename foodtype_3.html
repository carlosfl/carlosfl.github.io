  • Bourbon chicken

Description

This delectable chicken is bourbon styled and is highly recommended. It serves 1-2

Needed items

  • 1 lb Chicken filets
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Oil
  • Bourbon seasoning
  • 1/2 cup Water

Cooking steps

  1. Warm the oil in a pan over a low to medium heat
  2. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until browned
  3. Add the water, sugar, soy sauce, and bourbon seasoning
  4. Stir until the chicken is thoroughly seasoned
  5. Serve and eat
